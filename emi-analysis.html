Project Summary

Finding the source of switching noise.

This project examined how common-mode switching noise from an SMPS appeared in radiated emissions and how practical layout and shielding choices affected EMC behavior.

Equipment & Methods

  • Spectrum analyzer
  • Biconical antenna
  • SMPS emission testing
  • Shielding comparison
  • Cable twisting
  • Cable length reduction

Contributions

What I analyzed

Measurement

Measured radiated emissions across the 1-300 MHz range using a spectrum analyzer and biconical antenna.

Diagnosis

Identified dominant frequency components associated with common-mode switching behavior.

Mitigation

Applied shielding, cable twisting, and cable length reduction to compare emission reduction across configurations.
